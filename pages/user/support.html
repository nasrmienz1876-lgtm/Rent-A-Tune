Your Privacy at Rent A Tune

1. Data We Collect

At Rent A Tune, your privacy is our overture. We collect only the information necessary to facilitate seamless rentals and bookings — this includes contact details, instrument preferences, verification documents, and historical rental data used to personalize your stage experience.

payments

Secure Escrow Payments

All financial transactions are handled through our encrypted gateway. We use Secure Escrow Payments to ensure funds are only released once the instrument is delivered and the performance is complete, guaranteeing 100% security for both parties.

2. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only with verified owners and renters to fulfill the logistics of your booking, and with payment processors strictly to complete transactions. Your stage presence is your own; we just provide the platform.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and remember your preferences, and limited analytics cookies to understand how the platform is used so we can keep improving it. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser at any time.

4. Your Rights

  • Right to access and export your account data.
  • Right to request correction or deletion of non-transactional information.
  • Right to opt-out of promotional communications at any time.
  • Right to ask how your data is used in verification or fraud checks.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain account and transaction records only as long as needed for legal, tax, and dispute-resolution purposes. All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and access is limited to authorized Rent A Tune staff.

On-Platform Communication Only

All negotiation, booking, and payment discussions must happen through Rent A Tune's chat and checkout system. Sharing contact details to move a transaction off-platform voids Secure Escrow protection and may result in suspension.

2. Accounts & Verification

Users and gear owners must provide accurate registration information and complete our identity verification process. Accounts found to be fraudulent, impersonated, or shared without authorization may be suspended or terminated.
